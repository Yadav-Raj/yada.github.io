My websites

Here I discuss my websites that were made under company guidelines and time constrains.

Kaamcha

I was the web developer and brand manager for a startup called Kaamcha Nepal where we created Nepal's first freelancing website that allowed for people to work and hire freelancers which was not possible beforehand as international freelancing websites did not allow for the linking of Nepali Bank Accounts.

During my time at the company, we were able to create jobs worth 2 million and were invited to various national conferences for entrepurenuership. We gathered over a 1,000 freelancers in a short span of time before I had to move to the United States in the pursuit of my education. I worked closely with the three co-founders of the company and still am best friends with them.
